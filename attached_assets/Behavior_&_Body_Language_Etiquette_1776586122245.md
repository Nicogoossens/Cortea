# Database Schema: Behavior & Body Language Etiquette

## 1. Punctuality & Time Management
- **ID:** `BEH_001`
- **Field: Arrival_Buffer**
  - *Values:* Sharp (on the dot), Social buffer (15-30 min), or Fluid (1+ hour).
- **Field: Meeting_Conclusion**
  - *Values:* Hard stop vs. Open-ended (rushing is rude).

## 2. Personal Space & Physicality
- **ID:** `BEH_002`
- **Field: Proximity_Zone**
  - *Values:* Measurement in cm for Intimate, Social, and Public circles.
- **Field: Physical_Contact_Permissiveness**
  - *Values:* High (hugging/touching), Medium (handshakes), Low (no contact).
- **Field: Gender_Interaction_Rules**
  - *Values:* Specific boundaries for cross-gender interactions (e.g., in Saudi Arabia).

## 3. Posture & Gestures
- **ID:** `BEH_003`
- **Field: Sitting_Etiquette**
  - *Values:* Crossed legs (showing soles?), slouching vs. upright.
- **Field: Hand_Gestures_Dictionary**
  - *Values:* Local meanings of common signs (e.g., thumbs up, OK sign).
- **Field: Pointing_Protocol**
  - *Values:* Use of index finger vs. whole hand vs. chin/eyes.

## 4. Social Dynamics
- **ID:** `BEH_004`
- **Field: Queuing_Behavior**
  - *Values:* Strict linear queue vs. "First-come-first-serve" crowd.
- **Field: Gift_Exchange_Ritual**
  - *Values:* Two-handed giving, timing of opening (immediate vs. private), taboo gifts.
- **Field: Public_Display_of_Affection (PDA)**
  - *Scale:* 1 (Forbidden) to 10 (Normality).

## 5. Respect & Hierarchy
- **ID:** `BEH_005`
- **Field: Eye_Contact_Authority**
  - *Values:* Direct (trust) vs. Lowered (respect for elders/superiors).
- **Field: Elevator/Door_Etiquette**
  - *Values:* Gender-based vs. Rank-based priority.
