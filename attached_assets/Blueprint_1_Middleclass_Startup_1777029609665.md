# Blueprint 1 — Middle Class: Startup Structure
*This document defines what every country needs to build Phases 1, 2 and 3.*
*It is the minimum viable dataset before any country goes live.*

---

## The Four Universal Research Pillars

Every country's Phase 1, 2 and 3 is built on these four pillars.
The pillar names and research focus are identical for every country.
Only the examples, cultural references and historical context change per country.

| Pillar | English Term | Research Focus |
|--------|-------------|----------------|
| 1 | Adaptive Linguistics | Mixing respect with approachability |
| 2 | Professional Branding | Visual cues of reliability |
| 3 | Social Navigation | Behaviour in semi-public spaces |
| 4 | Merit-based Etiquette | Showing success through competence |

---

## Phase 1 — Individual Core

**Purpose:** How does a person navigate their own social and professional identity?
**Scope:** Universal across all countries. Cultural examples vary.

### Structure per country

```
Phase 1: [Pillar Name]
Bracket: [Demographic] — Level [N]: [Level Name]
10 Questions — Format: Question / A (Good) / B (Slightly different) / C (Would not do that) / Historical Context
```

### Demographics (identical for every country)

- Common (age-neutral baseline)
- Men 19–30
- Women 19–30
- Men 30–50
- Women 30–50
- Men 50+
- Women 50+

### Levels (identical for every country)

| Level | Name | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | The Foundation | Core entry-level rules |
| 2 | The Social Arena | Group and peer dynamics |
| 3 | Material Modesty | Money, possessions, status signals |
| 4 | High-Stakes Diplomacy | Conflict and negotiation |
| 5 | Crisis & Reputation Management | Recovery and resilience |

### Estimated questions Phase 1 per country
7 demographics × 5 levels × 10 questions = **350 questions**

## Phase 2 — Interaction Dynamics

**Purpose:** How do different demographics interact with each other?
**Scope:** Universal structure. Cultural context varies per country.

### Structure per country

```
Phase 2: [Demographic A] vs. [Demographic B] | Level [N]: [Level Name]
10 Questions — same format as Phase 1
```

### Interaction pairs (identical for every country)

- Men (19–30) vs. Women (19–30)
- Women (19–30) vs. Men (19–30)
- Men (30–50) vs. Women (30–50)
- Women (30–50) vs. Men (30–50)
- Men (19–30) & Women (30–50)
- Women (19–30) & Men (30–50)
- Men (30–50) & Women (50+)
- Women (30–50) & Men (50+)
- Men (50+) — solo
- Women (50+) — solo
- Men (50+) vs. Women (50+)
- Women (50+) vs. Men (50+)

### Levels Phase 2 (identical for every country)

| Level | Name |
|-------|------|
| 1 | The Sovereign Foundation |
| 2 | The Social Arena |
| 3 | Professional Authority & Material Modesty |
| 4 | The Legacy & Subtle Sovereignty |
| 5 | Transcendence & International Diplomacy |

### Estimated questions Phase 2 per country
12 pairs × 5 levels × 10 questions = **600 questions**

## Phase 3 — Social Navigation

**Purpose:** How does a person behave in semi-public spaces where private and professional overlap?
**Scope:** Universal structure. Country examples and historical context are localised.

### Structure per country

```
Phase 3: The Social Arena
Bracket: [Demographic] — Level [N]: [Level Name]
10 Questions — same format as Phase 1
```

### Demographics Phase 3

Same as Phase 1 plus interaction pairs:
- Common
- Men 19–30 / Women 19–30 / Men 30–50 / Women 30–50 / Men 50+ / Women 50+
- Men-Women 19–30 / Women-Men 19–30
- Mixed groups 30–50 / Mixed groups 50+

### Levels Phase 3 (identical for every country)

| Level | Name | Focus |
|-------|------|-------|
| 1 | Common | Basic semi-public navigation |
| 2 | Advanced Common | Complex social dynamics, institutional trust |
| 3 | Professional / Notable | Authority in mixed settings |
| 4 | Advanced Professional / Guardian | High-stakes public situations |
| 5 | Master of the Arena (The Sovereign) | Full social mastery |

### Estimated questions Phase 3 per country
10 demographics × 5 levels × 10 questions = **500 questions**
