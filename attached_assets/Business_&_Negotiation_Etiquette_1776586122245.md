# Database Schema: Business & Negotiation Etiquette

## 1. Professional Introduction
- **ID:** `BUS_001`
- **Field: Business_Card_Ritual**
  - *Values:* Two-handed exchange (Asia) vs. Casual swap vs. Digital only.
- **Field: Physical_Contact_Business**
  - *Values:* Firm handshake vs. Light touch vs. No contact (Bow).

## 2. Negotiation Style
- **ID:** `BUS_002`
- **Field: Decision_Speed**
  - *Values:* Rapid-fire (USA) vs. Consensus-based/Slow (Japan - 'Ringi').
- **Field: Conflict_Tolerance**
  - *Values:* Direct confrontation allowed vs. "Saving Face" (Never say no directly).
- **Field: Bargaining_Culture**
  - *Values:* Expected (Haggling) vs. Fixed price (Professional insult).

## 3. Meeting Dynamics
- **ID:** `BUS_003`
- **Field: Smalltalk_Duration**
  - *Values:* Zero (Get to work) vs. 30+ minutes (Building trust/relationship).
- **Field: Hierarchy_Presence**
  - *Values:* Only the boss speaks vs. Everyone contributes.
- **Field: Agenda_Strictness**
  - *Values:* Linear (Step-by-step) vs. Circular (Multi-topic jumping).

## 4. Corporate Gift Giving
- **ID:** `BUS_004`
- **Field: Gift_Appropriateness**
  - *Values:* When to give (First meeting vs. Closing) and what to avoid (Knives, clocks, certain colors).
- **Field: Bribery_Sensitivity**
  - *Values:* Legal/Ethical boundaries and local expectations.

## 5. Follow-up & Relationship
- **ID:** `BUS_005`
- **Field: Post_Meeting_Protocol**
  - *Values:* Immediate "Thank You" email vs. Waiting for the host to move first.
- **Field: Entertainment_Business**
  - *Values:* Importance of Karaoke/Dinner/Golf for closing deals.
