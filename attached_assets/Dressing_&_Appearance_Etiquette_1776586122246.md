# Database Schema: Dressing & Appearance Etiquette

## 1. Dresscode Translation
- **ID:** `DRS_001`
- **Field: Local_Interpretation**
  - *Context:* Business Casual, Smart, Black Tie (per country definition).
- **Field: Event_Specific_Dress**
  - *Values:* Weddings, Funerals, Gala, Business Meeting.

## 2. Cultural & Religious Sensitivity
- **ID:** `DRS_002`
- **Field: Modesty_Requirements**
  - *Values:* Coverage of shoulders, knees, chest, and hair.
- **Field: Religious_Symbols**
  - *Values:* Wearing vs. displaying external religious items (Crosses, Hijab, etc.).

## 3. Color & Material Symbolism
- **ID:** `DRS_003`
- **Field: Mourning_Colors**
  - *Values:* Black (West) vs. White (parts of Asia) vs. Purple (parts of LatAm).
- **Field: Forbidden_Colors/Patterns**
  - *Values:* Colors reserved for royalty or political affiliations.
- **Field: Fabric_Restrictions**
  - *Values:* Local taboos (e.g., Leather in specific Hindu contexts).

## 4. Status & Accessories
- **ID:** `DRS_004`
- **Field: Branding_Philosophy**
  - *Values:* "Loud" luxury (logos) vs. "Quiet" luxury (quality/no logos).
- **Field: Jewelry_Norms**
  - *Values:* Acceptable amount of jewelry for men/women in professional settings.
- **Field: Watch_Etiquette**
  - *Values:* Importance of timepiece as a status marker.

## 5. Grooming & Hygiene
- **ID:** `DRS_005`
- **Field: Facial_Hair_Policy**
  - *Values:* Clean-shaven vs. well-groomed beard (status/reliability).
- **Field: Fragrance_Intensity**
  - *Values:* Strong scent (Middle East/France) vs. Fragrance-free (Japan).
- **Field: Footwear_Protocol**
  - *Values:* Shoes on/off inside, sock quality/cleanliness.
- **Field: Tattoo_Visibility**
  - *Values:* Accepted vs. Must-cover.
