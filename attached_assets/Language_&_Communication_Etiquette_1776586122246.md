# Database Schema: Language & Communication Etiquette

## 1. Social Interaction (The Icebreaker)
- **ID:** `SOC_001`
- **Field: Address_Titles** - *Values:* Formal (Academic/Noble/Status) vs. Informal (First name).
- **Field: Greeting_Verbal**
  - *Values:* Specific phrases linked to physical actions (e.g., Bowing phrases in JP).
- **Field: Introduction_Order**
  - *Values:* Hierarchy rules (Age/Gender/Rank).
- **Field: Smalltalk_Matrix**
  - *Safe_Topics:* List of approved subjects.
  - *Taboo_Topics:* Sensitive subjects to avoid.

## 2. Register & Tone (Social Upgrade)
- **ID:** `REG_002`
- **Field: Grammatical_Formality**
  - *Values:* Existence of T/V distinction (U/Je, Tu/Vous, etc.).
- **Field: Directness_Index**
  - *Scale:* 1 (High Context/Indirect) to 10 (Low Context/Direct).
- **Field: Volume_Threshold**
  - *Values:* Public vs. Private decibel norms.
- **Field: Refusal_Codes**
  - *Values:* Local ways to say "No" without using the word.

## 3. Gastronomy (Dining Talk)
- **ID:** `GAS_003`
- **Field: Staff_Address**
  - *Values:* Correct terminology for service personnel.
- **Field: Toasting_Protocols**
  - *Values:* Verbal cues for drinking and order of speech.
- **Field: Complaint_Etiquette**
  - *Values:* Phrases for subtle dissatisfaction.
- **Field: Topic_Transition**
  - *Values:* When to switch from social to business talk.

## 4. Non-Verbal Context (The Silent Language)
- **ID:** `NON_004`
- **Field: Silence_Tolerance**
  - *Values:* Acceptable duration of silence (Seconds).
- **Field: Interruption_Norms**
  - *Values:* Allowed/Disallowed/Sign of engagement.
- **Field: Eye_Contact_Rules**
  - *Values:* Duration and intensity based on status.

## 5. Digital Etiquette (The Modern Script)
- **ID:** `DIG_005`
- **Field: Correspondence_Salutations**
  - *Values:* Opening/Closing formulas per hierarchy.
- **Field: Response_Latency**
  - *Values:* Expected timeframes for replies.
- **Field: Emoji_Semantics**
  - *Values:* Local interpretations of digital symbols.
