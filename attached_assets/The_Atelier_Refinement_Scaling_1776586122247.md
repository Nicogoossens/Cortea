# Database Schema: The Atelier Refinement Scaling

## 1. Pillar Weights
- **Pillar_1_The_World_Within:** Weight 1.0 (Cognitive/Knowledge)
- **Pillar_2_The_Presence:** Weight 1.2 (Visual/Physical)
- **Pillar_3_The_Voice:** Weight 1.5 (Auditory/Diplomatic)
- **Pillar_4_The_Table:** Weight 1.8 (Social/Complexity)
- **Pillar_5_The_Cellar:** Weight 2.0 (Expert/Sensory)

## 2. Global Ranking Tiers
- **Tier 1: The Aspirant** (0 - 50,000 AC)
- **Tier 2: The Observer** (50,001 - 250,000 AC)
- **Tier 3: The Practitioner** (250,001 - 1,000,000 AC)
- **Tier 4: The Connoisseur** (1,000,001 - 5,000,000 AC)
- **Tier 5: The Grandmaster** (5,000,000+ AC)

## 3. Engagement Loops
- **Daily_Refinement_Streak:** Bonus multiplier starts at 1.05x, caps at 1.5x after 30 days.
- **Micro_Correction_Penalty:** Failing a real-time audio nudge results in -50 AC (encourages focus).
- **The_Atelier_Challenge:** Weekly global events in specific countries (e.g., "Monaco Week") with 5x AC rewards.

De "Social Leaderboard"
Met 500.000 gebruikers kun je per regio (bijv. "De meest verfijnde Europeaan in China") leaderboards maken. Dit wakkert de competitiedrang aan tussen de klassen. Een "Middenklasse" gebruiker kan door pure inzet hoger op de ranglijst komen dan een "Upperclass" gebruiker die lui is met de regels.
